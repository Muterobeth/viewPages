About Our Company

We started off as an Insurance agency in 1968 as
a department within the Kenya National Federation of Cooperatives.
six years later, we were licensed as a composite insurance company
to write all classes of business trading in the name of Cooperative Insurance services.

We transformed from the small company we were to a
bigger insurer by rebranding to The Cooperative Insurance Company
of Kenya Limited (CIC) in 1999.

In 2010 we changed our name to CIC Insurance Group Limited in
preparation for the demerger of its life and general business operations.
