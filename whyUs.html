Why Us

CIC Group has for more than five decades shouldered the risks of Kenyans by
providing flexible insurance and financial services built around their needs.
With time, we have innovatively created customized products that have positioned us
as a trusted insurance and financial services provider.

Our positioning is simplified and captured in our tagline “We Keep Our Word”
reinforcing our commitment to the market.

Our expertise has been recognized over the years, winning awards as the AKI Group
Life Company of the Year since 2015, the Best Motor Insurer at the
Cheki Awards 2020, and the Decade of Excellence Insurance Kenya 2021 Award.
With decades of excellence and a strong corporate brand, CIC Group serves a wide range of
customers from Cooperatives to Corporates, SMEs, NGOs, Government entities, and individuals.
